Log Decrement → Damping Ratio

\(\delta = (1/n)\ln(x_1/x_{n+1})\), \(\zeta = \delta/\sqrt{4\pi^2+\delta^2}\)

Damping Ratio from Free Decay

Press Calculate to see result.
Multi-cycle method: Measure peaks 1 and n+1 — more accurate for lightly damped systems. Use n ≥ 5 for ζ < 0.02.

Forced Vibration Amplitude (SDOF)

\(X = (F_0/k)/\sqrt{(1-r^2)^2+(2\zeta r)^2}\) where \(r = \omega/\omega_n\)

Steady-State Response Amplitude

Press Calculate to see result.
At resonance (r=1): X = F₀/(2ζk). Low damping (ζ=0.02) → 25× static deflection.
